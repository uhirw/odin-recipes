Buffalo Chicken Mac and Cheese

DESCRIPTION

THis dish is made of Buffalo Chicken Mac and Cheese

This is also among the dishes that was requested the most and and i hope you'll get to enjoy it with your loved ones

INGRIDIENTS USED

  • Pasta
  • Butter and Flour
  • Milk (and sometimes Cream)
  • Cheese
  • Chicken
  • Buffalo Sauce
  • Seasonings

STEPS TO MAKE THE RECIPE

  1. Cook Pasta: Cook the macaroni pasta according to package directions, then drain.
  2. Make Roux and Sauce: Melt butter, whisk in flour to make a roux, then gradually whisk in milk to create a thick sauce.
  3. Melt Cheese: Reduce heat and stir in the shredded cheeses and seasonings until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
  4. Prepare Chicken: Toss the cooked and shredded chicken with your desired amount of buffalo sauce.
  5. Combine: Add the cooked pasta and buffalo chicken to the cheese sauce and stir until everything is well coated.
  6. CServe (or Bake): Serve immediately, or transfer to a baking dish, top with extra cheese and/or blue cheese crumbles, and bake until bubbly.
