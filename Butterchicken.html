Butter chicken recipe

DESCRIPTION

This whole dish includes rice, butter chicken and chapati but the special/main meal is butter chicken

This is among the dishes that was requested the most and i believe that if you get to eat it you/ll enjoy it

INGRIDIENTS USED

  • garam masala
  • tumeric powder
  • chili powder
  • lemon juice
  • onion
  • tomato sauce
  • heavy cream

STEPS TO MAKE THE RECIPE

  1. Marinate the Chicken: Combine the chicken pieces with the yogurt, marinade spices
  2. ook the Chicken: Sear the marinated chicken pieces in a skillet with a little oil until they're browned and mostly cooked through. Remove the chicken and set it aside.
  3. Sauté Aromatics: In the same pot, melt the butter (and some oil)
  4. Simmer the Tomato Base: Add the tomatoes (sauce/purée/crushed) and the sauce spices (garam masala, chili powder, cumin, coriander, turmeric, sugar, salt)
  5. Finish the Gravy: Stir in the heavy cream to create the signature richness. If desired, blend the sauce until smooth before adding the cream.
  6. Combine and Serve: Return the cooked chicken to the gravy. Crush the fenugreek leaves (Kasuri Methi) between your palms and stir them into the sauce. Simmer briefly until heated through, then serve.
