INGRIDIENTS USED

  • Hamburger Buns (toasted)
  • Ground Beef (80/20 lean-to-fat ratio is common)
  • Salt and Black Pepper (to taste)
  • Lettuce Leaves
  • Tomato Slices
  • Cheese Slices (e.g., Cheddar or American, for a cheeseburger)
  • Condiments (Ketchup, Mustard, Mayonnaise)

STEPS TO MAKE THE RECIPE

  1. Form Patties: Gently mix the ground beef with salt and pepper, and form into patties slightly wider than the buns. Press a small dimple in the center of each.
  2. Toast Buns: Lightly butter the cut sides of the buns and toast them on a grill or skillet until golden.
  3. Cook Burgers: Place patties on a preheated grill or hot skillet and cook for 3-5 minutes per side until desired doneness is reached.
  4. Add Cheese (Optional): During the last minute of cooking, top the patties with a slice of cheese and cover to melt.
  5. Rest: Remove the cooked patties from the heat and let them rest for a few minutes.
  6. Assemble: Spread condiments on the buns, then layer the patty, lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles. Serve immediately.
