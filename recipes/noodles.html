STEPS TO MAKE THE RECIPE

  1. Prep: Cook noodles al dente (slightly undercooked) according to package. Drain, rinse with cold water (to stop cooking and remove excess starch), and set aside.
  2. Stir-fry Aromatics: Heat oil in a wok or large pan over high heat. Add garlic and/or ginger, stir-fry briefly until fragrant (about 30 seconds).
  3. Cook Protein (if using): Add your protein and stir-fry until mostly cooked. Remove and set aside.
  4. Stir-fry Veggies: Add the vegetables and stir-fry quickly until crisp-tender (about 1-3 minutes).
  5. Add Noodles & Sauce: Return the protein (if applicable) and add the cooked noodles and the sauce mixture.
  6. Toss & Serve: Toss everything quickly to coat and heat through, until the sauce has been mostly absorbed by the noodles (about 1-2 minutes). Serve hot.
