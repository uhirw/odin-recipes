Rice recipe

DESCRIPTION

This whole dish includes rice, butter chicken and chapati but the special/main meal is butter chicken

This is among the dishes that was requested the most and i believe that if you get to eat it you'll enjoy it

INGRIDIENTS USED

  • white rice
  • Carrots
  • Water or vegetable broth
  • Oil or butter
  • onion
  • A pinch of salt
  • heavy cream

STEPS TO MAKE THE RECIPE

  1. Wash the rice under cold water until the water runs clear to remove excess starch. Drain well.
  2. Heat oil or butter in a medium pot over medium heat. Add the carrots and sauté for 2–3 minutes until they soften and the oil turns slightly orange.
  3. Add the drained rice to the pot. Stir for 1 minute so the grains are coated in the carrot-infused oil.
  4. Pour in the water (or broth) and add salt. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  5. Once boiling, turn the heat to low and cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid. Cook for 15 minutes without opening the lid
  6. Remove from heat and let it sit (covered) for 5 minutes. Fluff gently with a fork and serve.
